Mosquitoes: With the presence of stagnant water and high humidity during the rainy season, mosquito populations rise, posing a significant threat to public health due to the transmission of diseases such as malaria. Termites: The dry and arid conditions in the northern regions attract termite colonies, leading to structural damage to homes and buildings over time.

Bioinsecticides , they are products of natural origin or even living organisms that also serve to control insects. They differ from synthetic insecticides in their natural origin, they are less aggressive against the environment, and they are not usually toxic to higher organisms and plants. They are also usually more effective since they prevent insects from developing resistance to them, which usually happens with chemical insecticides, especially when they are abused.

Maintain Cleanliness: Keep homes and establishments clean, free of food debris, and promptly dispose of waste in sealed containers. Eliminate Standing Water: Mosquito breeding grounds can be eradicated by removing stagnant water sources around homes, ensuring proper drainage. Seal Entry Points: Repair cracks, gaps, and holes in doors, windows, and walls to prevent pests from entering your premises.
